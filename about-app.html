AKAN NAME WEB APPLICATION

This web application is based on determining the Akan name of a user with regards to his or her inputs.
The user is therefore required to key in their Gender and their birthday from the form provided. This web application then calculates the day of the week of the user was born, then depending on their gender outputs it provides their Akan name.

AKan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
For instance: If you are a male Born on the first day of the week you will be named Kwasi but if you are a female you will be named Akosua.

To determine your Akan name, click on the form tab in the home tab to key in your details in the provided form.
